No Child

Left Hungry

Studies have shown that hunger impacts health and our ability to focus. It has negative effects on school-aged children.

Instead of focusing in class, they focus on what they can possibly eat. Hunger affects low sugar, which leads to fatigue and low energy levels -- and wreaks havoc on the ability to focus.
