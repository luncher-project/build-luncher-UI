Our big vision is to end hunger.

Luncher is a small step in helping end hunger at school.

Luncher is an organized donation system that allows for a school to braodcast the lunch fund needs of their students. Schools declare an amount of the donation that they would like to be fullfilled in order to provide lunches for those that go without. Donors are able to select schools that they would like to donate to.
